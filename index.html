About Us



Miracoli Furniture, Inc. is a family owned and operated business.
We are committed to bringing you into our family by offering you the best prices, best services and the most environmental form of reupholstering your patio furniture. We have over 29 years of experience in the manufacturing of aluminum outdoor furniture, providing us with a strong foundation in the techniques needed for customizing the restoration of your outdoor furniture.

Our services include re-sling, re-strapping, sandblast & powdercoat, welding as well as the manufacture of umbrella covers, cushions and parts. Our refinishing processes can restore and customize your outdoor furniture to look new again.

When you contact us, you will be assured to always speak to a representative that will answer all your questions and concerns. By working on a case-to-case basis, we commit to making your
experience effortless.

You are welcomed to call, e-mail, and/or request a proposal at any time. Because your inquiry is important to us, we commit to contacting you within 1 business day, if not immediately.
